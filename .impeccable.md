## Design Context

### Users
This product is for professional writers and other serious text workers who care about craft, focus, and quality. They use it for professional writing and AI-assisted writing in a desktop workspace context.

### Brand Personality
Refined, precise, professional. The interface should feel composed, intelligent, and quietly premium rather than flashy or playful.

### Aesthetic Direction
The visual direction should draw from Linear and Craft: crisp panel structure, restrained surfaces, strong editorial alignment, and modern desktop-tool clarity. Prioritize light mode for now, with dark mode planned later. Avoid anything that feels like a back-office admin panel, generic AI product UI, warm yellow palettes, purple accents, heavy gradients, or skeuomorphic details.

### Design Principles
1. Design for concentration first: remove decorative noise and keep the writing surface calm.
2. Let structure create hierarchy: use alignment, density, spacing, and separators instead of cards and shadows.
3. Feel premium through restraint: refined typography, cool neutrals, and sharp interaction states over obvious styling.
4. Keep AI present but secondary: assistant controls should support the writing workflow, not dominate it.
5. Make every surface feel intentional: no dashboard aesthetics, no generic "AI slop", no ornamental gradients.
